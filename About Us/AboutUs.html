Our Story


Since 1971, Dan Bam Restaurant has been serve traditional Korean Food for residents of Gangnam-gu, Seoul. Kim Kwon Bam, the owner and the founding father of this restaurant build this restaurant for social purposes.


This restaurant want to build every one in their morning to bright up their day. Thats why we serve the best and delicious food and beverages in this restaurant.
